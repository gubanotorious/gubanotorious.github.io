Bridge Identity Platform

Overview

Bridge Identity is a unique approach to sovereign identity that is ideal for securely managing, and transmitting digital identity. Users are empowered to be in total control and have the power to only disclose their identifying data when and with whom they choose. Since Bridge Identity is fully portable, it also provides several online, offline, and blockchain-based identity verification options with added flexibility to accept Bridge Identity in any application or real-world use case. The framework was built and open-sourced to bring sovereign digital identity to the community with the core SDK, integration components, and end-user clients available and free to use in all of your software and hardware applications.

What is Self-Sovereign Identity?

Sovereign as an adjective is defined as "possessed of supreme power", "unlimited in extent", and "enjoying autonomy", often used in reference to a supreme ruler or king. Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI) is the idea that individuals and organizations have complete ownership of their identity and complete control of their personal data. As we move to a more digital existence, being able to properly implement these concepts has become more important than ever. Some of the key components of making this possible in the digital realm are:

  • Persistence and Portability
    Users must have an independent existence. Identities must be long-lived. Information and services about identity must be transportable.
  • Control and Consent
    Users must control their identities. Users must have access to their own data and any associated claims without the interference of gatekeepers or intermediaries. Users must agree to the use of their identity. Disclosure of claims must be minimized. The rights of users must be protected.
  • Transparency and Interoperability
    Systems and algorithms must be transparent. Identities should be as widely usable as possible.

Bridge Passport

The Bridge Passport is the key to your digital identity. Just like a real-world passport would contain your personal information, the Bridge Passport is your container for your sovereign digital identity. A Bridge Passport contains your Bridge Identity cryptographic keys, your blockchain wallets and cryptographic keys for on-chain transactions, and verified information about your identity in the form of secure, portable digital claims.
